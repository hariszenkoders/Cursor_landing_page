Empowering students and parents to track their academic, personal, and professional accomplishments over time. Develop your personal “Folio”, upload your accomplishments, and track your goals. Profiles are kept private; however, young adults and parents have the option to share their “Folio” with recruiters and employers at their discretion in this all-in-one platform.

I’m Accomplished is Perfect for...

  • Parents tracking their child’s milestones.
  • Students tracking their accomplishments and goals in and outside of the classroom. We're a great tool to aid in the college recruitment and application process by streamlining a record of your accomplishments.
  • Athletes looking to share their Folio with recruiters.
  • Post-grads looking to build their Folio and document their experiences, look back on their accomplishments, and have a record of their progress.

Highlights and Tools:

  • Personalized “Folio”, a resume-like summary of your accomplishments.
  • 15+ categories of Accomplishments.
  • Thoughtfully curated sub-categories for applicable fields.
  • “Custom” Accomplishments.
  • Goal Tracking.
  • Printable “Folio” PDF.
  • Default privacy settings.
  • Users 13+ can share their Folio with recruiters/employers straight from the app.
  • An easy button to stop sharing and revert to a private account.
